ABOUT TRANSPARENT PROGRAMMING

Looking at any webpage through the lens of a programmer is quite interesting. Because, for every action, movement or process that is obtainable by interacting with such webpage a programmer can imagine what consists of the front-end or back-end. i.e when you click the submit button or click a link that opens a new tab. Its so clear to a programmer as if looking through a transparent media.

Speaking of programmers, I don't know if I can call myself one at this point where I am just about two months into the game. Although, I now see certain things differently but not with superb clarity. Yeah! In order words things become more transparent with time and experience. I am currently undergoing a mentorship program on web development with eHealth Africa Academy (eHAA) where I started with HTML, then CSS. The experience has been a "coded" one.
