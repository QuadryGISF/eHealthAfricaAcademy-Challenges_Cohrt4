Learn

As you traverse the length and breadth of the globe
notice how humankind try to do simply amazing things
in order to derive satisfaction from those things
that is where learning comes into play.

Learn whatever you want
especially things that will make you
when people move from grass to grace; they have learnt something in between
when someone goes from zero to hero; he has learnt something in between
from the quietness of space to the darkness of deep ocean
learning never ends.

As a rule of thumb
learn, unlearn and relearn.
